AnimateLCM: Accelerating the Animation of Your Personalized Models via Latent Consistency Models

Click to Play the Animations Generated with AnimateLCM in 4 steps!

Abstract

The field of video diffusion models has been gaining prominence for its ability to produce videos that are both coherent and of high fidelity. However, the iterative denoising process makes it computationally intensive and time-consuming, thus limiting its applications. Inspired by the Consistency Model that distills pretrained image diffusion models to accelerate the sampling with minimal steps and its successful extension Latent Consistency Model on conditional image generation, we propose AnimateLCM, reducing the sampling steps for high-fidelity video generation within four steps. To embrace the vast personalized image diffusion models and tools in Stable Diffusion community, we freeze the image base model and decouple the distillation of image generation priors and motion priors, which improve the training efficiency and enhance the generation visual quality. Additionally, understanding that one of the deep reasons for the success of stable diffusion community is the development and combination of plug-and-play tools to achieve various functions including controllable generation, editing, etc., we propose an efficient strategy that could directly tame the distilled text-conditioned video consistency model to accommodate existing tools or train them from scratch.
